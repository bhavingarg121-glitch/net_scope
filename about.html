About NetScope India

Who We Are

NetScope India is a technology-driven platform focused on providing network insights, telecom information, and digital connectivity tools for users across India. As the country rapidly expands its digital infrastructure through 4G, 5G, and broadband technologies, our mission is to make network intelligence accessible, transparent, and useful for everyone.

Our Mission

To help millions of Indians make informed decisions about their mobile and internet connectivity by providing accurate, user-friendly, and transparent network information.
